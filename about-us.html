Stock Market Prediction (SMP)

Stock Market Prediction (SMP) is a comprehensive platform designed to give users insight into the stock market through AI-driven predictive modeling.

It allows users to track their favorite stocks, add them to watchlists, maintain a virtual portfolio, and execute simulated trades based on predictions. We prioritize empowering absolute beginners with easily digestible knowledge in real-time.

The platform aims to demystify stock market trends using Machine Learning algorithms (like LSTM & Random Forest) alongside advanced real-time sentiment analysis using FinBERT. This helps traders, learners, and analysts to make highly informed decisions over traditional speculation.
